Angio Project

Developing a 3D phase-field model for tumor angiogenesis

About

This project is hosted by the Centre for Computational Physics at the University of Coimbra and consists in a phase-field model for tumor angiogenesis. The model is based on the paper published by Travasso et al. (2011) with some physical and computational improvements.
